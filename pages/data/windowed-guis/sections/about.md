{{section: Windowed GUIs}}

<center><em>Windowed GUIs turns Minecraft's full-screen container menus into draggable, OS-style windows. Open a chest, a furnace, and your crafting table at the same time, drag them around the screen, resize them, and manage everything from a Windows-style taskbar at the bottom of the screen.</em></center>

**How it works:** No more closing one GUI just to open another. Pin the windows you use most, minimize the ones you don't, merge related menus into tabbed groups, and keep your whole workflow on screen at once, keep multiple GUIs open at the same time.
