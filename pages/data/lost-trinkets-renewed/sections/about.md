{{section: Lost Trinkets Renewed}}

<center><em>Lost Trinkets adds in 70+ exceedingly rare unlockable trinkets with different useful abilities for you to play with. Trinkets completely change the way you play the game by giving you over-powered skills.</em></center>

**How to unlock trinkets:** Trinkets are randomly unlocked when doing activities like fighting enemies and bosses, trading, mining ores, cutting trees, and farming. Each activity has their own chance of giving you a trinket, some riskier activities may reward you sooner with goods. (Check the config file to tweak the different unlock chances to your liking)

**How to check enable/check your trinkets:** By default, you can access the trinket GUI by pressing the "R" key on your keyboard.
