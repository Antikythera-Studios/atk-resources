{{section: Hats Renewed}}

<center><em>Hats Renewed adds in more than 430+ collectible hats & accessories for players to unlock. Featuring rarity tiers, customizable colors, and unlockable accessories for you to tweak & change your hats to your liking.</em></center>

**How to unlock hats:** Hats are wearable cosmetics that can appear on mobs and be collected by players. When mobs spawn, a percentage of them are randomly assigned a hat, kill these mobs & collect their hats.

**How to equip/check your hats:** By default, you can access the Hats GUI by pressing the "H" key on your keyboard.
